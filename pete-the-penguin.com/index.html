About

About

Pete the Penguin is a first-person survival/action game for PC with additional VR mode available. The game takes place in Antarctica in the year 3000, where World War IV has just ended. The entirety of the world has been decimated by the war. The only land on Earth suitable for living now is Antarctica, which holds out a small colony of survived scientists and native penguins. Playing the role of Pete the penguin, the player must fight against the enemies created from the war and hold his ground against all odds.
